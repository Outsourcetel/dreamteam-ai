## What it is

The **General** tab of **Settings**, where you set your workspace's identity — its name, industry, the words the whole product uses for the people you serve, your Digital Employees' reply language and tone, contact email, and brand colour.

## Why it matters

These settings shape how the whole workspace looks and speaks. Your vocabulary relabels every page to match your business, your language and tone settings change how every DE answers, and your brand colour is applied across the interface.

## Before you start

Open **Settings** and stay on the **General** tab. Changes here apply to your whole workspace, so an admin should make them.

## Options & settings

**Workspace Details**

- **Workspace Name** — your organization's name, shown across the app.
- **Industry** — the industry your workspace is set to; the same canonical list used at sign-up and in the Company Setup wizard. Changing it here keeps your stored industry consistent with the templates.
- **Your vocabulary** — relabels the whole workspace to your business. Seeded from your industry, yours to change:
  - **You serve one…** — the singular term for who you serve (e.g. *Customer*, *Patient*, *Client*).
  - **…and many** — the plural form (e.g. *Customers*, *Patients*, *Clients*).
  - **Value metric** — your headline value measure (e.g. *ARR*, *Contract value*).
  - **Recurring commitment** — what you call a renewal (e.g. *Renewal*, *Contract*).
  - **DE reply language** — the language your Digital Employees answer in; every DE answer honors this.
  - **DE tone of voice** — the style your DEs use (e.g. *warm, concise, formal*); applied to every DE answer.
- **Contact Email** — the contact address for your workspace.
- **Brand Colour** — pick a colour (swatch or hex). It's applied to the sidebar, buttons, and highlights across the platform. A live preview sits beside the field.

Click **Save Changes** to apply. A confirmation appears on success; workspace name, industry, vocabulary, and brand colour take effect across the app right away.

**Trial banner** — if your workspace is on a trial, a banner at the top of this tab shows the trial end date. See [managing-your-plan-and-trial](managing-your-plan-and-trial.md).

**Danger Zone** — contains a **Delete Workspace** action. Treat it as irreversible and use it with care.

## The other Settings tabs (in brief)

The Settings page has several tabs; **General** is the one every admin uses:

- **AI Engine** — provider keys; visible to platform-team members only. See [configuring-the-ai-engine](configuring-the-ai-engine.md).
- **Usage & Budgets** — per-workspace monthly token limits. See [managing-your-plan-and-trial](managing-your-plan-and-trial.md).
- **Widget & API** — the public Q&A widget; live workspaces only. See [embedding-the-customer-widget](embedding-the-customer-widget.md).
- **Billing** and **Security** — placeholders today ("coming soon"). For real security controls — team, roles, sessions, access — use **Governance → Security & Access**, not this tab.

## Tips & best practices

- Set your **vocabulary** early — it makes every downstream page read in your own language and avoids retraining your team on generic labels.
- Use **DE tone of voice** to keep every employee's replies on-brand; a short instruction like "warm, concise, no jargon" goes a long way.
- Choose a **brand colour** with enough contrast against dark backgrounds, since it's used for buttons and highlights.

## Troubleshooting

- **"Save failed."** Usually a connection issue — check the error note and retry.
- **"I changed the industry but recommendations didn't change."** The industry field keeps your stored value consistent; recommended hires and guardrails are applied through the **Company Setup** wizard, not this tab.
- **"Where are real security settings?"** Under **Governance → Security & Access**. The **Security** tab here is a placeholder.
