## What it is

How plans, the 14-day trial, and token budgets work in DreamTeam AI — and what happens when a trial ends.

## Why it matters

Your plan controls how much AI work your workspace can do each month (its token budget). Knowing where your trial stands, and what "suspended" means, keeps your Digital Employees answering without surprises.

## The plans

Every workspace is on one of three plans, each with a standard monthly token budget:

| Plan | Standard monthly token budget |
|------|-------------------------------|
| **Starter** | 100,000 tokens / month |
| **Growth** | 500,000 tokens / month |
| **Enterprise** | 2,000,000 tokens / month |

New workspaces start on **Starter**. Tokens are the unit of AI usage — a single knowledge-grounded answer is a small fraction of your monthly budget. Usage resets on the 1st of each month.

## The 14-day trial

A brand-new workspace is created in **trial** status with a **14-day** clock. During the trial your workspace is fully functional. You can see where you stand in **Settings → General**: a banner reads *"Trial — ends [date]"* with a note that the workspace will be paused if the trial isn't upgraded by then.

## What happens when a trial ends

When the trial date passes, the workspace is **automatically suspended** (this runs once a day). A suspended workspace stops doing paid AI work:

- **Your Digital Employees stop answering.** The public Q&A widget returns a "workspace suspended" response instead of an answer, and DE answer paths stop.
- Your data and configuration are **not** deleted — everything is preserved. Suspension pauses the workforce; it does not erase your workspace.

## How to reactivate or upgrade

DreamTeam does not yet collect payment self-serve, and the in-app **Billing** tab is a placeholder ("coming soon"). Plan changes and reactivation are handled for you:

1. **Contact us to arrange a plan.** The trial banner and the AI-engine billing notes both point you to talk to us about a plan; billing is handled manually for now.
2. Once arranged, your workspace is moved onto the plan you choose, which sets that plan's standard monthly token budget. Your workforce resumes.
3. If you genuinely need more than your plan's standard budget, your budget can be raised for you beyond the default.

## Options & settings

- **Trial banner** — shown in **Settings → General** while your workspace is in trial, with the exact end date.
- **Token budgets** — the per-workspace monthly limit is managed in **Settings → Usage & Budgets**. When a workspace reaches its budget, its DEs stop responding until the monthly reset or a budget increase. The bar turns amber near the limit and red at 90%+.

## Troubleshooting

- **"My DEs suddenly stopped answering."** Two common causes: the workspace is **suspended** (trial ended), or it has hit its **monthly token budget**. Check the status in Settings; contact us to reactivate or raise the budget.
- **"The Billing tab is empty."** That's expected — self-serve billing isn't built yet. Reach out to arrange your plan.
- **"How much does a query cost?"** Very little — for example, on the cheapest model a typical short query costs on the order of a tenth of a cent. Budgets are set in tokens, not dollars.
