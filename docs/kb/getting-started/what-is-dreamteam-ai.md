## What it is

DreamTeam AI is an enterprise operating system for **Digital Employees** — AI workers that answer questions, prepare work, and carry out tasks for your customers and your staff, under your supervision.

It is not a chatbot and it is not another system of record. It is a **work-and-judgment layer that sits on top of the tools you already use** — your help desk, your CRM, your billing system, your knowledge — and puts a supervised digital workforce to work across them.

## Why it matters

Most "AI assistant" tools are a text box bolted onto a product. DreamTeam is built around the opposite idea: that an AI worker should behave like an employee you can actually trust with real work. That means every Digital Employee:

- **Answers from your knowledge, not from guesswork** — replies are grounded in the documents and sources you give it, and it cites them.
- **Works inside guardrails** — rules you set can block certain topics, phrases, or actions before they ever reach a customer.
- **Escalates when unsure** — when confidence is low or a rule is triggered, the work is handed to a human instead of guessed at.
- **Leaves an audit trail** — everything a Digital Employee does is recorded, so you can see exactly what happened and why.

Together, grounding, guardrails, and audit make up what we call the **Control Fabric** — the part of the product that turns raw AI into a workforce you can govern.

## Who it's for

DreamTeam is built for operators who want AI to do real work without giving up control:

- **Service and support teams** who field high volumes of repetitive questions and want first-contact answers drafted or resolved automatically.
- **Operations and back-office teams** who want reminders, follow-ups, reconciliations, and status-chasing handled consistently.
- **Regulated and compliance-sensitive businesses** — finance, healthcare, legal, and others — where an AI worker must stay strictly inside hard limits and route anything sensitive to a person.
- **Agencies and multi-client operators** who run separate, isolated workspaces for each customer they serve.

You configure your Digital Employees to your industry, connect the systems they work on, and decide how much they can do on their own. They handle the volume; your people handle the judgment calls that need a human.

## The core idea in one line

Your systems of record hold your data. DreamTeam adds the **workforce** that acts on that data — grounded, guarded, and fully audited — so you can scale service and operations without scaling headcount.
