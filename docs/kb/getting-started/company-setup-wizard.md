## What it is

A four-step wizard that gets your workspace working: pick your **Industry**, choose your **first Digital Employees**, turn on **starter guardrails**, and **review and create** them for real. It lives at **Setup → Company Setup**.

## Why it matters

Everything the wizard creates is real and editable — not a demo. When you finish, it saves your industry to your workspace, hires the Digital Employees you selected (as genuine employees that then earn their way to live work), and creates enforceable guardrail rules. It is the fastest way to go from an empty workspace to a supervised workforce.

## Before you start

- You must be in a **live workspace**. In a demo workspace the wizard is fully interactive but nothing is provisioned — a banner tells you so.
- Know roughly which functions you want help with first (support, renewals, operations, and so on). The industry you pick tailors the recommendations.

## Step by step

**Step 1 — Industry.** Choose the industry that best fits your business from the grid (Technology, Finance, Healthcare, Retail, Professional Services, Manufacturing, Education, Real Estate, Legal, or Other). Your choice sets the recommended first hires and the starter guardrail set, is saved to your workspace, and seeds your workspace vocabulary and pipeline stages — all editable later. Click **Continue**.

**Step 2 — First hires.** The wizard lists the recommended Digital Employees for your industry, each with its department, what it does, and why it's worth hiring first. Every one you keep becomes a **real** Digital Employee at the **Designed** lifecycle stage — it then earns live work through the same gates as any hire (knowledge, testing, your certification). Untick any you don't want. Employees already on your roster are shown as such and can't be duplicated. Click **Continue**.

**Step 3 — Guardrails.** The wizard lists the starter guardrails for your industry. Each rule shows its real matching detail underneath — the pattern it will match, or the dollar threshold for an approval rule — because these enforce from the moment they exist; they are not policy statements. Untick any you don't want. Rules that already exist in your workspace are shown as such. Click **Continue**.

**Step 4 — Review & create.** Review the summary: the industry that will be saved, the new employees that will be hired, and how many new guardrails will be created. Click **Finish setup — create for real**.

When it completes, you see a confirmation and two shortcuts: **Meet your employees** (the Roster) and **Review guardrails** (Compliance & Guardrails).

## Options & settings

- **Deselecting hires or rules** simply narrows what gets created — you can always add more later from the Roster or the Compliance & Guardrails page.
- **Re-running the wizard** is safe. It never duplicates an employee or rule that already exists; it only fills in what is missing.
- **Guardrail detail** shown in Step 3 is the real enforcement pattern. For example, an approval rule displays its threshold in dollars; a blocked-topic rule displays the text fragments it matches.

## Tips & best practices

- Start lean: hire the one or two employees that cover your highest-volume work, and add more once you have knowledge in place for them.
- Keep every recommended guardrail on unless you have a specific reason to remove one — they cost nothing until they trigger.
- After finishing, go straight to **Knowledge → Library** and give your new employees something to answer from.

## Troubleshooting

- **"Finish" won't create anything.** You are in a demo workspace, or no industry is selected. Switch to your live workspace and pick an industry first.
- **A recommended hire is greyed out.** It already exists on your roster — no action needed.
- **Setup failed with an error.** Nothing partial is left behind for the failed step; fix the issue noted and run the wizard again — it will only create what is still missing.
