## What it is

A guided tour of the left-hand sidebar. DreamTeam is organized around the Digital Employee, so the sidebar groups everything by what an employee *knows*, *touches*, *does*, and *is supervised by*.

## Why it matters

Once you know what lives in each section, you can find any feature quickly. The sidebar is grouped into labelled sections; you can collapse it to an icon rail with the arrow at the bottom, and switch between your live workspace and demo companies from the picker at the top.

## The sections

**Command Centre**
Your home dashboard — the at-a-glance view of what your workforce is doing: activity, key numbers, and what needs attention. Start here each day.

**Digital Employees**
Everything about the workforce itself:
- **Roster** — your Digital Employees; create, open, and configure each one.
- **DE at Work** — a live view of what employees are doing right now.
- **Performance** — resolution rate, confidence, escalations, errors, and cost per employee.
- **Outcomes** — the business results your workforce is producing (economics, delivery, risk).
- **Proving Ground** — evaluations that test a DE's answers before you trust it with more.
- **Self-Learning** — patterns detected from humans correcting a DE's decisions.
- **Insights** — cross-cutting observations about your workforce.
- **Specialist Desk** — read-only consult desks (Technical, Legal, Finance, People) that answer strictly from configured sources.

**My Tasks**
Your human-in-the-loop queue:
- **Approvals & Drafts** — work a DE has prepared or escalated for a person to approve. A badge shows how many are pending.
- **Activity Log** — a running record of what has happened.

**Knowledge**
What your employees answer from:
- **Library** — the documents in your knowledge base.
- **Ingestion & Sources** — bring in documents, PDFs, and pages.
- **Gap Detection** — questions your knowledge base can't yet answer well. A badge shows open gaps.
- **Quality & Coverage** — how well-grounded and well-cited your answers are.

**Playbooks**
- **Playbook Builder** — build and edit the step-by-step procedures your DEs follow.

**Connectors**
- **Systems & Actions** — connect your existing systems (help desk, CRM, billing, and more) and configure what a DE can read or do in each.

**Company Data**
The business the employees work on top of. This section is labelled with *your* vocabulary — for example **Customers**, **Patients**, or **Clients** — and expands into the lifecycle pipelines: Business Development, Sales, Onboarding, Support, Success, and Renewal & Expansion. Each shows a live count of what's in play. This is the substrate your DEs act on, not the product itself.

**Governance**
The Control Fabric, made visible and editable:
- **Compliance & Guardrails** — the rules that block or route sensitive work.
- **Audit Trail** — the durable record of everything that happened.
- **Security & Access** — team, roles, sessions, and access controls.
- **Data Access** — which employees can see which data.
- **Identity & Credentials** — the inventory of stored credentials.
- **Trust & Architecture** — how trust and the platform's controls fit together.

**Setup**
- **Company Setup** — the four-step wizard for choosing your industry, first hires, and starter guardrails. See [company-setup-wizard](company-setup-wizard.md).

## Options & settings

- **Collapse the sidebar** with the arrow at the bottom to get an icon-only rail; the top eight areas stay one click away.
- **Workspace picker** (top of the sidebar) switches between your live workspace and the built-in demo companies, and offers **Add company**.
- **Account menu** (bottom) is where you change your password and sign out. **Contact support** is also there.

## Tips & best practices

- Some sections only show what your role is allowed to open — if you don't see a page, your access level may not include it.
- Sections with pending work show a coloured badge (pending approvals, open knowledge gaps) — use them as your daily to-do signal.
