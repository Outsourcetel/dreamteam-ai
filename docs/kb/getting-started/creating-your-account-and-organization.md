## What it is

The first-run flow that turns a brand-new sign-up into a live workspace: creating your login, confirming your email, setting up your organization, and receiving the starter workforce and rules DreamTeam provisions for you.

## Why it matters

Sign-up is deliberately split into two parts — creating a **login** and creating an **organization**. Your account is created first; your workspace is created only after you confirm your email and sign in. This keeps unverified emails from provisioning real workspaces, and it means the moment your organization *is* created, it comes pre-loaded with a working starter setup rather than an empty shell.

## Before you start

- A work email you can receive mail at (you must confirm it).
- Your organization's name and its closest industry — both are asked for, and both are easy to change later.

## Step by step

1. On the sign-in screen, click **Create Account**.
2. Fill in **Full Name**, **Work Email**, a **Password** (at least 8 characters), your **Organization Name**, and your **Industry**, then click **Create Organization**. This creates your login only — no workspace exists yet.
3. You will see **"Organization created — check your email to confirm your account."** Open the confirmation email and click the link.
4. Return to the sign-in screen, switch to **Sign In**, and log in with your email and password.
5. Because your account has a login but no organization yet, you land on the **"One last step"** screen. Confirm or edit your **Organization Name** and **Industry** (these are pre-filled from what you typed at sign-up) and click **Create My Workspace**.
6. Your live workspace is created and you are taken into it. From here you can run the **Company Setup** wizard to choose your first Digital Employees and guardrails.

## What gets auto-provisioned

The moment your workspace is created, DreamTeam seeds a working baseline so you are never starting from nothing:

- **Two starter Digital Employees** plus their playbooks, charters, trust policies, and default automation settings.
- **A starter guardrail pack** — around seven enforceable rules covering frustration signals (for example, "speak to a manager" or a cancellation threat), a block on unilateral refund promises, a block on legal-threat language, and an approval requirement on actions over $10,000.
- **A starter onboarding template** — a published 10-step implementation checklist (kickoff → data → config → validation → go-live) with human sign-off gates.
- **A Technical Specialist desk** — a read-only consult profile that answers only from configured sources and escalates when unsure.

**Connectors are the one thing not seeded**, because they need your own system credentials. Connecting your tools is a guided setup step you do yourself.

Your new workspace also starts on the **Starter** plan with a **14-day trial**. See [managing-your-plan-and-trial](../settings-billing/managing-your-plan-and-trial.md).

## Options & settings

- **Industry** sets which starter employees and guardrails the Company Setup wizard recommends. Choose the closest fit — you can change it in **Settings → General** at any time, and pick **Other** if nothing matches.
- **Organization Name** becomes your workspace name and is used to generate a unique workspace address. It is editable later in **Settings → General**.

## Troubleshooting

- **"I signed in but I'm asked to set up an organization again."** Your login exists but the workspace step did not finish. Complete the **"One last step"** screen — it is the only place a workspace gets created.
- **I never got the confirmation email.** Check spam, and confirm you entered the right address. You must confirm before you can sign in.
- **Forgot your password?** Use **Forgot password?** on the sign-in screen to receive a reset link.
