## Strategic Thesis

**Phase 1 Mission (North Star, Doc 13):** Transform Outsourcetel into the world's leading AI-native managed services company by deploying a specialized Digital Employee for every operational domain.

This roadmap IS Phase 1. Instead of 30 shallow demos, build 10 **genuinely expert** digital employees FOR Outsourcetel's own operations. Each role = **archetype** (persona + playbooks + connectors + knowledge + guardrails + eval + pricing). Once built and certified at Outsourcetel, the configuration becomes reusable and licensable to external customers in Phase 2.

**Customer #1:** Outsourcetel (internal operations, Weeks 1-36)  
**Customers #2+:** External organizations licensing proven playbooks (Week 36+)

**Success Metric (Phase 1, at Outsourcetel):** Organization-wide measurable impact across all 10 operational domains:
- Support (20-30% cost savings via automation)
- Sales (2-3x productivity per rep via ops automation)
- Finance (40-50% of back-office work automated + certified correct)
- Operations (3-5x faster onboarding, implementation, contract processing)
- Marketing (autonomous campaign optimization, keyword research, bid management)

**This is not a pilot. Outsourcetel runs its own operations on DreamTeam for 36 weeks. Real data proves the machinery works. That proof becomes the basis for selling to other organizations.**

## 1. THE 10 ROLES (Tiered by Complexity & Market Readiness)

### TIER 1: PROOF-OF-CONCEPT (Immediate — Weeks 1-12)
These roles have highest market demand, proven connectors, clear success metrics.

#### 1. **Support Agent** (Customer Service)
- **Core Problem Solved:** Handle ticket routing, troubleshooting, FAQs, escalation
- **Key Connectors:** Zendesk, Salesforce Service Cloud, Intercom, HubSpot
- **Knowledge Sources:** Product KB, help articles, previous tickets, FAQs
- **Machinery Required:** Bounded consultation (mig 111), decision trace (mig 161), escalation rules (DE-B2)
- **Certification Criteria:** 70% first-contact resolution on golden-set (20 tickets, mixed complexity)
- **Success Metrics:** Cost per ticket ↓20%, CSAT ↑15%, resolution rate 65-70%
- **Go-Live Timeline:** Week 8 (Outsourcetel internal proof, then market)
- **Market Size:** $30B/yr (incumbent Fin, Zendesk)
- **Competitive Advantage:** Transparent decision traces (Fin doesn't expose these)
- **Archetype Status:** SHIPPED (mig 111-162 live)

#### 2. **Specialist: Billing & Invoicing**
- **Core Problem Solved:** Process invoices, reconcile AR/AP, follow-up on overdue accounts
- **Key Connectors:** QuickBooks, Stripe, FreshBooks, Bill.com, NetSuite
- **Knowledge Sources:** Billing policies, tax rules, payment terms, customer history
- **Machinery Required:** Deterministic correctness (mig 163 routing), gated writes (mig 154), compliance packs (mig 166), audit trail (mig 161)
- **Certification Criteria:** 100% accuracy on invoice processing (reconciliation against source data), zero payment errors on golden-set
- **Success Metrics:** AP/AR cycle time ↓30%, errors ↓95%, audit-ready decision traces
- **Go-Live Timeline:** Week 12 (after Support proof)
- **Market Size:** $15B/yr Finance/Accounting (zero competition)
- **Competitive Advantage:** Audit-grade decision traces + compliance packs (nobody else has)
- **Archetype Status:** IN DESIGN

#### 3. **Sales Operations Agent**

- **Key Connectors:** Salesforce, HubSpot, Apollo, LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Outreach
- **Knowledge Sources:** Sales playbook, customer history, competitive intel, pricing rules

## 9. COMPETITIVE MOAT (Why This Works)

### Why Competitors Can't Copy
1. **Audit Trails + Compliance** (migs 161-166)
   - Took 6 months to build; Fin/Sierra/Decagon avoid regulated domains
   - Cannot add retrospectively without architectural rewrite

2. **Amendment Loop + Replay Testing** (Wave 7, migs 161)
   - Unique to DreamTeam; competitors ship static agents
   - Replay testing framework is 12+ weeks to build from scratch

3. **Deterministic Routing** (mig 163)
   - Model selection based on task complexity
   - Competitors optimize for single model; you route intelligently

4. **Certification Gating** (mig 162)
   - Golden-set evals before autonomy
   - Market leaders avoid gating (slows deployment)

5. **Multi-Hop Bounded Consultation** (mig 111)
   - Allows DE-to-DE routing with context chaining
   - Competitors escalate to human or route to one other bot

6. **Structured Knowledge** (Living Documents)
   - Amendment bridge into playbooks + knowledge updates together
   - RAG-only competitors degrade at scale

### Time-to-Copy: 12-18 Months
- Audit trails: 6 months
- Replay testing: 12 weeks
- Certification gating: 8 weeks
- Learning loop: 8 weeks
- Total: ~6-9 months for infrastructure, +6 months for role specialization maturity

**Your window: 12-18 months before serious competition.** Use it to dominate regulated domains (finance, medical, insurance) and become the standard for certified DEs.
